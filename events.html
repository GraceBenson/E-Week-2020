ENGINEERING FAIR (EFAIR)

February 11, 2020 - February 12, 2020
9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Reitz Student Union

The Engineering Fair is targeted towards the youngest generation of students, namely those in grades K-12. With attendance of over 2,000 students from 30 different counties, companies are invited to present their story and experience with the generation of the future!

ESL'S INSPIRE TO ASPIRE

February 13, 2020
10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Reitz Student Union North Lawn

This event is held on behalf of the ESL (service) team, apart of UF's Engineer's Week 2020. Our team will be setting up stations with different activities that all students can participate in. The activities will be then be donated to KidCity USA, a local chapter that allows toddlers and children to advance their studies at a younger age. The activities will be STEM-related, but the event is open to all!

ENGINEERING LEADERSHIP FORUM (ELF)

February 13, 2020
5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
Arredondo's Cafe

Engineering Leadership Forum (ELF) introduces speakers from every field of engineering (mechanical, electrical, civil, environmental, industrial and biomedical) who will provide insightful information about their experience in the engineering world. They'll also answer any questions you may have about their specific field of work!

DODGEBALL TOURNAMENT

February 13, 2020
7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Flavet Field

E-Week's 2020 Dodgeball Tournament is a 5 vs 5, double elimination game hosted by E-Week's fitness department. It will be fun and a good time to relieve, to relax, and to vent some stress. Just remember the 5 D's of dodgeball: Dodge, Duck, Dip, Dive & Dodge!

It costs $10 to enter, and all proceeds (a minimum of $200) will be given to the winner to go towards their organization's DM page.

Venmo $10 to @eweek_2020 with your team members' names and organization to sign up!

BOWLING WITH FACULTY

February 14, 2020
6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Reitz Union Game Room

Bowling with Faculty provides a unique opportunity for all students, professors, and advisors at the University of Florida. Students get the chance to know some of the most influential people in the field of engineering and broaden their networks in a night of free fun and games, while faculty members can get to know their students and share their experiences in a more casual setting than a classroom or office. Take this opportunity to expand your network and explore new horizons all while enjoying free bowling!
Please bring your Student ID to this event!
